We are a trio from Seattle, Washington that makes pop-infused rock n roll. We are Marika and Miro (sisters) and Toby, who we have known since middle school. Our influences include The Clash, Sky Ferreira, Mazzy Star, Charli XCX, the Pixies, and more. Obsessed with 80s inflected guitars, complex instrumentals, and pop melodies, we’re somewhere at the crossroads of indie and mainstream, influenced as much by fashion, film, and pop culture as we are by our favorite musicians.

Since forming in 2013, we’ve been lucky enough to be featured in NME, Nylon Indonesia, The Guardian, Impose, She Shreds, KUOW NPR, MTV Buzzworthy, Rookie Mag, Tom Tom Magazine, The Seattle Times, The Philadelphia Daily, news and more. We have also had original music appear in MTV’s Awkward, Fox’s Cooper Barrett’s Guide to Survival, and ABC’s The Real O’Neals.

We are currently writing and recording our first full-length record in Seattle and Los Angeles.
